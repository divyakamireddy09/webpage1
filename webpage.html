FACT SHEET: How the Inflation Reduction Act Helps AA and NHPI Communities

August 19, 2022

Lower Health Care Costs

The Inflation Reduction Act (IRA) will lower health care costs, including prescription drug costs, and expand health insurance coverage for AA and NHPI families.

  • Lower Prescription Drug Costs for Seniors: Capping costs for prescription drugs and insulin.
  • Expanding Coverage: Providing more affordable health insurance coverage.

Combat Climate Change and Lower Energy Costs

The Inflation Reduction Act takes aggressive action on climate and clean energy, reducing costs and creating jobs while addressing climate impacts on vulnerable communities.

  • Home Efficiency Upgrades: Offering tax credits for energy-efficient appliances and improvements.
  • Job Creation: Expanding clean energy job opportunities in disadvantaged communities.

Economic Opportunities and Jobs

The Act provides tax credits for businesses adopting clean energy, benefiting small business owners and creating jobs in the process.

Make the Tax Code Fairer

The Inflation Reduction Act will help ensure that high-income individuals and corporations pay their fair share of taxes, without increasing taxes for those earning under $400,000.
